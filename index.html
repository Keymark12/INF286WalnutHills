Area Council



The Walnut Hills Area Council was established in 1965 as the voice to represent the interests of the community. We are recognized by the City of Cincinnati as entity for Walnut Hills.



Our Mission


The mission of WHAC is to provide a safe, stable, diverse community where we live, learn, work , pray , play and prosper together



The Vision


Walnut Hills will be a safe, healthy, attractive, educated, caring community with a sense of pride and respect for both individuals and community. Our leaders will be strong, well organized and able to advocate for the rights of all citizens.

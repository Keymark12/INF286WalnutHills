business group

The neighborhood of Walnut Hills, Cincinnati boasts a rich variety of businesses, from long-standing community icons to newcomers, including bars and restaurants, live music venues, hair salons and boutiques, as well as specialized services like commercial signage, custom framing, interior design and architectural services. Read about our businesses in the news!


The mission of the Walnut Hills Business Group is to promote opportunities for sustainable business development and growth in a safe and attractive environment.


Area business owners are encouraged to read the Walnut Hills Business Group minutes and apply for Walnut Hills Business Group membership.
